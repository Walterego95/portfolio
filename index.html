ABOUT ME

Walter is a young junior developer driven by a constant thirst for knowledge. He was born in Palermo, Italy. Moved to the United States in 2015, he came to this country to pursue all his dreams and to achieve his personal goals for career and freedom. His background and experience goes from the hospitality industry to the Cryptocurrency, Distributed Ledger Technologies (DLTs) and Financial Markets. One of the main reasons he decided to pursue the path of software developer, was driven by his passion for the groundbreaking concepts of the DLT industry and the future of a cash-less society with fast, fair and secure deployment of Decentralized applications to empower the people without a need a centralized third-party.
